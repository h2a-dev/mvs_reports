## APR Component Definitions

- **Supply Rate**: Base interest rate from borrowers at the Liquidity Layer
- **Rewards Rate**: Additional interest from native protocol rewards (if active)
- **Staking APR**: Native asset staking yield (e.g., wstETH staking rate)
- **Merkle APR**: Additional rewards from Fluid merkle programs
- **Total APR**: Cumulative APR = Supply Rate + Rewards Rate + Staking APR + Merkle APR

*Note: All rates converted from basis points (1 bp = 0.01%). Total APR is the sum of all components.*
